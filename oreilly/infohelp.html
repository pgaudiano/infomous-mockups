The Twitter Navigator

The Twitter Navigator is based on the Infomous interactive text cloud technolgy. This cloud provides a visual summary of Tweets.

Word size indicates relevance; the larger the word, the more relevant it is to stories posted on the site throughout the day. Only the 40 or so most relevant words appear. Related terms and concepts are linked together with lines.

Click a word to show links to relevant stories on the site. Click any link to open that link in a new page. Click anywhere to close the list. Double-click on a word to focus content around it. Focus mode places that word in the center of the screen and rearranges the rest of the cloud so that only those words related to the focus word remain in view. To exit focus and return to overview mode, click the pushpin icon.
