Infomous Live Demos

This page offers links to a handful of live demos showing Infomous in action. Clicking any link will open the corresponding demo in a new window or browser tab.
  • Today's World News from Infomous. This is our main page for browsing world news, an example of a News Navigator.
  • USA TODAY News Navigator (scroll to the bottom of the article). This is another News Navigator, in this case showing how USA TODAY can help readers find news from its entire web site in a convenient, interactive fashion.
  • USA TODAY 2013 in Review. An example of our Time Machine, which lets you browse, week-by-week, top stories on USA TODAY from 2013.
  • The 2014 Nantucket Conference in review. Another example of our Time Machine, which lets you browse tweets during the 2014 Nantucket Conference.
  • 2014 World Cup on Twitter. This is a sample Social Spotlighter based on my personal passion for the World Cup. Click team names to see tweets related to that team.
  • NFL Films. A different kind of social spotlighter - actually a list of ten pages, capturing social media related to the top ten most buzzworthy moments of the 2013 season. This is the centerpiece of a special episode of NFL Top 10 that will air in late summer of 2014.
