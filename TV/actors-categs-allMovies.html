Actors and categories (for all movies)

This cloud shows relationships between actors based on movies they've co-starred in. The data represents all movies from the New York headend on Saturday Feb 26 2011 starting at or after 8pm.

Movie categories are shown in blue. The relative sizes of words are based on their frequency. Removing all category words will emphasize which actors star in more movies. A cloud with all categories removed can be viewed here.

Things to play with:

  • Click any actor name to see a list of movies they star in, complete with description and categories
  • Click the "Comedy" category (or over actors from its group) and then click on the green "Comedy" link; this will open a cloud of the same format but with data from comedies only.
  • Find "Billy Bob Thornton" in the "Romance" group and click it; after the description of the movie "Indecent Proposal" there is a blue "IMDb" link; click it to go to the IMDb page for the movie.
  • Find "Jennifer Aniston" and click it; the "He's just not that into you" movie title is a link - click it; this will open a cloud focused on this movie
  • Uncheck "Groups" - more links will show up
  • Double-Click on an actor (e.g. Bruce Willis) to focus on them: all actors connected to them will show up
    • Note that when clicking a related actor or category the popup list now shows only the movies also casting the focused actor.
    • Click anywhere in the cloud background to unfocus and see the original cloud again
